About

SMS Scheduler is a simple tool for sending SMS automatically.

Features

  • Choose message text from pre-defined templates
  • Add custom messages to templates
  • Add your own groups
  • Recipients can be typed in directly or selected from contacts
  • Choose multiple recipients to send messages
  • View history of sent and delivered messages
  • Enter messages using Google voice
  • Messages are classified as Scheduled, Sent or Draft
